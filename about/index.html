Our Mission

The door hardware industry relies on standards, codes, and technical specifications that are often locked behind paywalls or written in language that requires expert interpretation. Architects, contractors, and building owners frequently need quick, reliable answers to questions like:

  • What hinge do I need for this fire-rated door?
  • How do I meet both ADA and fire code requirements on the same door?
  • What does ANSI/BHMA A156.17 Grade 1 actually require?
  • How do I adjust closing speed after installation?

We created the Door Hinge Knowledge Hub to be the definitive, freely accessible resource that answers these questions — accurately, completely, and in plain language. Our content is written by engineers and door hardware professionals with decades of hands-on experience.

What We Cover

  • Hinge Types Encyclopedia — Complete guides to every type of door hinge: butt hinges, spring hinges, self-closing hinges, continuous hinges, pivot hinges, concealed hinges, and swing clear hinges.
  • Standards & Code Compliance — Plain-language explanations of ANSI/BHMA A156.17, NFPA 80, UL fire ratings, ADA, ICC A117.1, and grade classifications.
  • Application Guides — How to select and specify hinges for commercial buildings, healthcare, education, fire-rated doors, glass doors, and exterior gates.
  • Technical References — Sizing guides, material comparisons, hydraulic vs spring mechanisms, closing speed adjustment, and troubleshooting.
  • Installation & Maintenance — Step-by-step installation, maintenance schedules, hinge replacement, and required tools.
  • Industry Resources — Glossary, standards directory, and industry organization directory.
  • Blog — In-depth articles on door hardware engineering, code compliance, and material science.

About Waterson Corporation

Waterson Corporation has been manufacturing door hinges since 1979. Headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia with manufacturing facilities in Taiwan, Waterson specializes in investment-cast self-closing hinges with patented hydraulic speed control technology.

Key Differentiators

  • Investment casting — All hinge components are precision investment cast (lost-wax process) for superior grain structure, tighter tolerances, and smoother operation compared to stamped hinges.
  • Hydraulic speed control — Waterson's flagship hybrid technology combines spring force with hydraulic dampening in a single hinge barrel, enabling field-adjustable closing speed that meets both ADA and fire code requirements.
  • UL-listed for 3-hour fire rating — The highest available fire-rating for self-closing hinges, suitable for any fire-rated door application.
  • ANSI/BHMA A156.17 Grade 1 — Rated for 1,000,000+ open-close cycles.
  • ISO 9001 certified — Quality management system covering design, manufacturing, and testing.
  • TAA compliant — Manufactured in Taiwan, eligible for U.S. government and GSA procurement.
