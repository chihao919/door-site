Installation & Maintenance

Everything you need to install, maintain, troubleshoot, and replace door hinges — from basic residential butt hinges to commercial self-closing hinges on fire-rated doors. Includes step-by-step procedures, maintenance schedules, tool lists, and code-compliant installation requirements.

Installation & Maintenance Guides

  • Door Hinge Installation Guide

    Step-by-step installation procedures for butt hinges, spring hinges, and hydraulic self-closing hinges. Includes hinge placement measurements, fire door requirements, and post-installation verification.

  • Maintenance Schedule

    Recommended inspection and maintenance intervals by environment type: standard commercial, high-traffic, exterior, and healthcare. Includes lubrication guide and NFPA 80 annual fire door inspection requirements.

  • Hinge Replacement Guide

    How to identify, measure, and replace door hinges. Covers upgrading from standard butt hinges to self-closing hinges and fire door hinge replacement requirements.

    Complete tool checklist for hinge installation and maintenance: essential tools, specialty tools for self-closing hinges, and professional vs homeowner recommendations.

  • Troubleshooting Guide

    Systematic diagnosis and repair for common hinge problems: sagging doors, squeaking, doors not closing, slamming, oil leaks, and seasonal adjustment issues.
