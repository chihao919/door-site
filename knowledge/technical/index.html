Technical References

In-depth technical guides for door hardware professionals — sizing, mechanisms, materials, and adjustments.

These references are written for architects, specifiers, contractors, and facilities managers who need precise technical detail — not marketing summaries. Each guide covers the underlying mechanics, field adjustment procedures, and comparative performance data needed to make informed hardware decisions and troubleshoot installations.
