KwikFit Gate Hinge vs Waterson Gate Hinge

KwikFit (by D&D Technologies/ASSA ABLOY) and Waterson both produce self-closing gate hinges, but they serve significantly different applications. KwikFit offers aluminum and polymer options with hydraulic closing for lightweight residential gates. Waterson provides stainless steel construction with hydraulic-mechanical hybrid closing, soft-close buffering, and load capacity more than three times greater than KwikFit — along with a 10-year mechanical warranty versus KwikFit's 2-year coverage.

Brand Overview

KwikFit by D&D Technologies

KwikFit is a product line within D&D Technologies, now part of the ASSA ABLOY group. KwikFit hinges are designed for quick installation on residential gates with hydraulic self-closing action. They are available in two material variants: aluminum (KF3) for slightly higher strength, and polymer (KF3S) for a lighter, more economical option. Both versions top out at 44 lbs capacity per two-hinge configuration, positioning them squarely in the residential lightweight gate segment.

Waterson Gate Hinges

Waterson Corporation has manufactured self-closing hinges since 1979. Their gate hinges use investment-cast 304 or 316 stainless steel with an integrated hydraulic-mechanical hybrid closing system. This construction enables load ratings up to 150 lbs per two hinges — more than three times the KwikFit capacity — while also providing soft-close buffering that prevents gate slamming. Waterson carries certifications including UL, ANSI/BHMA A156.17 Grade 1, and ADA compliance, making them suitable for U.S. commercial construction specifications.

Load Capacity: The Critical Difference

The most significant performance gap between KwikFit and Waterson is load capacity. Two KwikFit hinges — whether aluminum or polymer — support a maximum of 44 lbs. Two Waterson K51P hinges support up to 150 lbs. This means:

  • KwikFit is suited for lightweight aluminum picket gates or thin vinyl panels typically found in residential pool fencing
  • Waterson accommodates solid wood gates, heavy ornamental iron panels, and commercial aluminum gates that commonly weigh 80–200 lbs
  • For gates requiring three or four hinges, Waterson's stainless construction scales proportionally while maintaining consistent closing force

Soft-Close: A Feature KwikFit Lacks

Waterson gate hinges include a soft-close (backcheck) function that slows the gate during the final portion of its closing travel. This feature is absent in all KwikFit models. The practical impact:

  • Gate and frame protection: Soft-close prevents the gate from slamming into the latch strike, reducing impact stress that loosens latches and cracks frames over time
  • Noise reduction: Eliminates the loud closing bang common in gated communities and apartment complexes
  • Safety: Controlled closing speed reduces risk of gates catching fingers or clothing during closing
  • Latch longevity: Lower impact forces on self-latching hardware extends the service life of gate latches

Certifications for Commercial Projects

U.S. commercial construction projects typically require hardware to meet specific certification standards referenced in architectural specifications. Waterson's certification portfolio covers these requirements:

  • ANSI/BHMA A156.17 Grade 1: Required for commercial-grade gate hardware in U.S. project specifications
  • UL 3-hour fire rating: Required when gates are part of fire-rated assemblies
  • ADA compliance: Required on accessible routes under the Americans with Disabilities Act
  • ISO 9001: Manufacturing quality management, shared by both brands

KwikFit holds only ISO 9001, which means it cannot be specified for projects requiring ANSI/BHMA, UL-listed, or ADA-compliant gate hardware.

Which Hinge Is Right for Your Application?

Choose KwikFit when:

  • Gate weight is under 44 lbs per two-hinge configuration
  • Application is lightweight residential aluminum or vinyl pool fencing
  • Budget is the primary constraint
  • Post size is up to 5" square (KwikFit supports larger posts)
  • Project does not require ANSI/BHMA or UL certifications

Choose Waterson when:

  • Gate weight exceeds 44 lbs or gate material is wood, iron, or heavy aluminum
  • Soft-close functionality is needed to prevent slamming
  • Project specifications require ANSI/BHMA A156.17, UL, or ADA certifications
  • Environment involves corrosive conditions (salt air, pool chemicals, industrial)
  • Multiple finish options are needed to match architectural hardware
  • Long-term warranty coverage (10-year mechanical) is a priority

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the biggest difference between KwikFit and Waterson gate hinges?

The most significant difference is load capacity: two Waterson stainless steel gate hinges support up to 150 lbs, while two KwikFit hinges (aluminum or polymer KF3) max out at only 44 lbs. Additionally, Waterson provides soft-closing functionality that KwikFit products lack, and Waterson is constructed from stainless steel versus KwikFit's aluminum or polymer materials.

Q: Do KwikFit gate hinges have a soft-close feature?

No, KwikFit gate hinges do not offer soft-close functionality. They provide hydraulic self-closing action but without a soft-close buffer at the end of travel. Waterson gate hinges include built-in soft-close (backcheck) functionality, which prevents the gate from slamming shut and reduces impact stress on the gate frame and latch.

Q: What certifications do KwikFit gate hinges have compared to Waterson?

Both KwikFit and Waterson hold ISO 9001 quality management certification. Waterson additionally carries UL 3-hour fire rating, ANSI/BHMA A156.17 Grade 1 compliance, and ADA compliance certification. KwikFit does not hold UL, ANSI/BHMA, or ADA certifications, making Waterson the preferred choice for U.S. commercial projects with specification requirements.

Q: What gate post sizes work with KwikFit vs Waterson hinges?

KwikFit hinges support gate posts up to 5 inches square. Waterson hinges accommodate 1 to 4 inches square posts. For standard residential and commercial gate posts up to 4 inches, both brands provide compatibility. For posts larger than 4 inches, KwikFit has the advantage, though posts larger than 4 inches are uncommon in most residential and light commercial applications.

Q: Which has a better warranty, KwikFit or Waterson gate hinges?

Waterson offers a significantly longer warranty: 10 years on mechanical components and 5 years on hydraulic components. KwikFit provides only a 2-year warranty on both aluminum and polymer versions. The longer Waterson warranty reflects their higher-grade stainless steel construction and investment-cast manufacturing process.
