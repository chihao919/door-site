The Challenge: Heavy Gate Applications Requiring ADA Compliance

Commercial retail establishments and industrial facilities increasingly require gate hinges that must simultaneously handle high gate weights and meet ADA compliance standards. Traditional heavy-duty hinges address one requirement or the other — rarely both.

A typical commercial retail gate specification might require:

  • Gate dimensions: 29"×36", weighing 160 lbs
  • Loading capacity: 180–200 lbs per pair (2 hinges per gate)
  • ADA compliance: 5-pound maximum opening force
  • Self-closing function for security and pool safety code compliance

Waterson's stainless steel weld-on heavy duty gate hinges address this challenge through an enhanced-strength design that combines high load capacity with adjustable self-closing force.

For gates heavier than the rated capacity, additional non-functional hinges (without the closing mechanism) can be added to provide structural support while keeping the active closing hinges within their rated capacity.

ADA Compliance for Gate Hardware

Gates on ADA-accessible routes must meet specific force and operation requirements. ADA Standards for Accessible Design and ICC A117.1 specify:

  • Maximum opening force: 5 lbf for gates on accessible routes
  • Self-closing requirement: Gates must not require sustained force to remain open
  • Clear width: Minimum 32" clear opening at 90° position

Waterson heavy duty gate hinges are adjustable to meet the 5-pound opening force requirement while still providing reliable self-closing function — satisfying both ADA accessibility requirements and pool safety code compliance simultaneously.

Pool Safety Code Compliance (IRC AG105.2.8)

Pool barrier gates require self-closing and self-latching hardware under IRC AG105.2.8. This safety requirement prevents unsupervised access to pool areas. Waterson self-closing gate hinges:

  • Automatically return the gate to the closed and latched position
  • Resist wind and casual pressure that might hold a gate open
  • Provide adjustable closing force to ensure reliable closure in all conditions
  • Meet the hydraulic damping requirement to prevent slam closing

Adjusting Waterson Heavy Duty Gate Hinges

Field adjustment of Waterson gate hinges allows precise tuning after installation:

  1. Spring tension adjustment — Controls closing force; increase for more aggressive closing, decrease to meet ADA 5 lbf limit
  2. Hydraulic speed control — Controls closing speed; slow closing prevents slamming and meets ADA timing requirements
  3. Test and verify — Verify opening force with a door force gauge; verify closing speed with a stopwatch
  4. Lock settings — Secure adjustment screws once optimal settings are achieved

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much weight can heavy duty steel gate hinges hold?

Waterson heavy duty steel gate hinges support over 260 lbs with three hinges and up to 440 lbs with four hinges. For gates exceeding these capacities, additional non-functional hinges can be added to further distribute the load and strengthen the assembly.

Q: Are heavy duty gate hinges required to be ADA compliant?

Gates on accessible routes must meet ADA requirements, including a maximum 5 lbf opening force. Waterson heavy duty gate hinges feature adjustable closing speed and spring tension, allowing installers to set the opening force below the ADA 5-pound threshold while still providing reliable self-closing function for pool safety code compliance.

Q: What is a weld-on gate hinge and when is it used?

A weld-on gate hinge is welded directly to the gate frame and post rather than screwed or bolted into place. This method provides maximum strength for heavy industrial gates, metal frame gates, or applications where screw-mounting is impractical. Waterson's offset barrel design accommodates full-surface and mid-post mounting configurations.

Q: What building codes apply to heavy duty gate hinges?

Heavy duty gate hinges for pool barriers must comply with IRC AG105.2.8, which requires self-closing and self-latching gates. ADA-accessible gates must meet 5 lbf maximum opening force. Waterson heavy duty steel gate hinges are UL Grade-1 certified (one million cycle tested), meeting both IRC pool safety and ADA accessibility requirements.

Q: How do you adjust Waterson heavy duty metal gate hinges?

Waterson gate hinges are adjusted by modifying spring tension and hydraulic closing speed using the adjustment screws on the hinge body. The spring tension controls the closing force, while the hydraulic control adjusts the speed. Both settings can be fine-tuned in the field after installation to achieve the desired opening force and closing speed for ADA compliance and code requirements.
