TruClose Gate Hinge vs Waterson Gate Hinge

TruClose (by D&D Technologies/ASSA ABLOY) and Waterson are two leading brands in self-closing gate hinges. TruClose uses plastic polymer construction with UV resistance, targeting residential pool and fence gates. Waterson uses heavy-duty 304/316 stainless steel with hydraulic closing control, supporting up to 330 lbs across four hinges — nearly double the TruClose Heavy Duty capacity. This comparison covers material, load capacity, gate post compatibility, certifications, and warranty.

Brand Overview

TruClose by D&D Technologies

TruClose is a product line by D&D Technologies, now owned by ASSA ABLOY — one of the world's largest lock and door hardware groups. TruClose specializes in self-closing gate hinges designed primarily for residential pool fencing, residential security gates, and lightweight commercial fence gates. Their polymer construction offers UV resistance and corrosion resistance without the weight or cost of metal hardware.

Waterson Gate Hinges

Waterson is a family-owned manufacturer based in Taiwan with U.S. distribution through Watersonusa.com. Founded in 1979, Waterson produces ISO 9001-certified self-closing hinges using investment-cast stainless steel. Their product line covers both mechanical spring-tension and mechanical-hydraulic hybrid closing mechanisms, targeting commercial, industrial, and high-end residential gate applications.

Material: Stainless Steel vs Plastic Polymer

The most fundamental difference between Waterson and TruClose hinges is material. This single factor drives most of the performance differences:

Why Material Matters for Gate Hinges

  • Load capacity: Stainless steel investment castings support far greater structural loads than polymer housings, enabling heavier gate panels
  • Corrosion resistance: 316 stainless steel (marine grade) resists chlorine, salt spray, and pool chemicals indefinitely. Polymer degrades under prolonged UV and chemical exposure
  • Temperature stability: Polymer hinges can soften in extreme heat or become brittle in extreme cold, affecting closing consistency
  • Vandal resistance: Stainless steel resists tampering and forced entry attempts better than polymer
  • Aesthetics over time: Stainless steel maintains appearance; UV-stabilized polymer may yellow or discolor with age

For residential pool fencing where gates are lightweight (under 55 lbs) and budget is a priority, TruClose polymer hinges are a practical and widely accepted choice. For commercial installations, heavier gates, or environments with chemical or salt exposure, the stainless steel Waterson hinges provide longer service life.

Gate Post Compatibility

TruClose hinges are designed for residential fence posts, typically 1.5"–2" square tubing common in aluminum and wrought-iron residential fencing. Waterson hinges accommodate a wider range of post sizes from 1" to 4" square, including the heavier structural tubing used in commercial ornamental iron gates, industrial access gates, and security fencing.

Certifications and Standards

Both brands hold certifications relevant to their target markets:

  • TruClose: AS1926-12 (Australian pool safety standard), the relevant standard for residential pool gate compliance in Australia and New Zealand. Widely accepted for pool fence permit compliance in many U.S. jurisdictions.
  • Waterson: UL 3-hour fire rating, ANSI/BHMA A156.17 Grade 1 (U.S. commercial hardware standard), ADA compliant, ISO 9001 manufacturing quality certification.

For U.S. commercial construction projects requiring ANSI/BHMA compliance or fire-rated hardware specifications, Waterson certifications align with standard U.S. project specs. TruClose hinges are better suited for residential pool code compliance projects.

Which Hinge Is Right for Your Application?

Choose TruClose when:

  • Gate weight is under 55 lbs (Regular) or under 154 lbs (Heavy Duty)
  • Application is residential pool fencing requiring AS1926-12 compliance
  • Budget is the primary constraint for a residential project
  • Post size is standard residential (1.5"–2" square)
  • Lifetime warranty on polymer components is preferred

Choose Waterson when:

  • Gate weight exceeds 150 lbs or you need more than two hinges
  • Environment involves salt air, pool chemicals, or industrial corrosives
  • Project requires ANSI/BHMA A156.17, UL, or ADA certifications
  • Post size is 3"–4" square (commercial/industrial tubing)
  • Multiple finish options are needed to match gate design
  • Vinyl gate support is required

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the main difference between TruClose and Waterson gate hinges?

The primary difference is material construction. TruClose gate hinges are made from plastic polymer, while Waterson gate hinges are manufactured from heavy-duty 304 or 316 stainless steel. This leads to significant differences in load capacity, corrosion resistance, and long-term durability, especially in harsh outdoor or pool environments.

Q: Which gate hinge has a higher weight capacity, TruClose or Waterson?

Waterson gate hinges have significantly higher weight capacity at the four-hinge level. Two Waterson K51P hinges support up to 150 lbs, and four hinges can hold up to 330 lbs. In comparison, two TruClose Regular hinges support up to 55 lbs, and TruClose Heavy Duty tops out at 154 lbs with two hinges.

Q: Are TruClose gate hinges suitable for pool gates?

TruClose hinges are commonly used for pool gates and meet AS1926-12 pool safety standards. They are UV-resistant and designed for outdoor use. However, for saltwater or highly corrosive pool environments, Waterson stainless steel hinges offer superior long-term corrosion resistance due to their 304/316 stainless steel construction.

Q: What gate post sizes are compatible with TruClose vs Waterson hinges?

TruClose gate hinges are compatible with gate posts up to 2 inches square. Waterson gate hinges accommodate a wider range, from 1-inch to 4-inch square posts, making them more versatile for commercial-grade metal and wood gate installations.

Q: Which brand offers better warranty coverage for gate hinges?

Waterson offers a 10-year warranty on mechanical components and a 5-year warranty on hydraulic components. TruClose Regular hinges do not provide a stated warranty, while TruClose Heavy Duty offers a lifetime warranty. For hydraulic longevity, Waterson's 5-year hydraulic warranty reflects the complexity of their integrated hydraulic closing mechanism.
